# Security Policy

Security is of the utmost importance to us. If you have discovered a security vulnerability in our project, please 
follow the guidelines below to report it to us.

## Reporting a Vulnerability

- **Do not** publicly disclose the vulnerability without prior coordination with us.
- If you have found a vulnerability, please email us at [email address] with a description of the vulnerability and 
  any relevant details.
- We will respond to your email as soon as possible and will work with you to verify and reproduce the vulnerability.
- Once we have confirmed the vulnerability, we will take steps to fix it as quickly as possible.

## Responsible Disclosure

We support and follow the principle of responsible disclosure. This means that we will work with you to understand and 
resolve the issue before any public disclosure.

## Rewards

We appreciate the efforts of those who responsibly disclose security vulnerabilities to us. The ultimate reward for 
your contributions will be eternal glory in the commit history, where your name will be forever etched as a security 
guardian of the project. In addition, we will be adding them to the acknowledgement section of this document.
